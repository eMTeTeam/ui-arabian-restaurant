About Us

“The Arabian restaurant founded by Mr. Abdul Hameed at the year of 2015 in Tuticorin. This is the first restaurant introduces Shawarma at Tuticorin city to fulfill the people hunger as a happiest moment. The restaurant started with two menu items are as Shawarma and Grilled chicken.

Right from there, In 2016 with people support then the restaurant improvised with 10 members seated facility. Based on the people feedback the new menu items Briyani and Tandoori items are added. Along with that Lunch facility is also added up.

In 2018 – The restaurant improved into Multi Cuisine Restaurant with 50 members seating capacity as part of people service.

In 2021– Mandi introduced as part of new menu item in the Arabian Restaurant, at first time in Tuticorin. For people service the seating capacity is increased in to 100 members and Special family rooms are also added to make the delightful Lunch/Dinner.

The Arabian Restaurants’ motto to make people taste buds are as the happier one always. The hospitality for the customer can be considered as a main goal of the Restaurant. Maintaining the High quality and taste of a food is the vision of Arabian Restaurant in upcoming years.

Take away facility available without set up for any minimum order amount. Happy to serve you better always with delicious food at Ontime!! “
